Espresso

A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, forming a layer of foamy cream (also known as crema) on top.

Latte

A smooth and creamy drink made with one part espresso and three parts steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of milk foam. It is milder in taste than a cappuccino.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian drink made of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a thick layer of milk foam, giving it a rich, frothy texture and a balanced flavor.
